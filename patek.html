INFO

Patek Philippe is one of the oldest and most prestigious Swiss watch manufacturers, renowned for its tradition of excellence, innovation, and craftsmanship. Founded in 1839 in Geneva by Antoni Patek and later joined by Adrien Philippe, the brand remains a symbol of horological artistry. Uniquely, Patek Philippe is still family-owned, currently run by the Stern family, ensuring independence and continuity in design and philosophy.

Patek Philippe is celebrated for its technical mastery and timeless elegance. It has introduced numerous milestones in watchmaking history, such as the first Swiss wristwatch (1868), the Perpetual Calendar mechanism, and the Annual Calendar. Signature models include the Calatrava, known for its understated refinement; the Nautilus, a luxury sports watch introduced in 1976; the Aquanaut, a modern, youthful take on the Nautilus; and the Grand Complications series, which showcases the brand's mastery in perpetual calendars, minute repeaters, and split-second chronographs.
