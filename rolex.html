INFO

Rolex is a prestigious Swiss watchmaker and one of the most recognized names in luxury timepieces. Founded in 1905 in London by Hans Wilsdorf and Alfred Davis as Wilsdorf and Davis, the company moved to Geneva in 1919. Today, it remains privately owned by the Hans Wilsdorf Foundation, which reinvests profits into the brand and charitable initiatives.

Rolex is known for innovation and timeless design. It introduced several landmark models: the Datejust (1945), the first watch with an automatic date change; the Submariner (1953), a pioneering diving watch; and the GMT-Master (1955), designed for pilots tracking multiple time zones. Other iconic models include the Daytona for motorsports, the Explorer for adventurers, the Sky-Dweller, with dual time and an annual calendar, and the newly introduced Land-Dweller (2025), which features a high-frequency movement, integrated bracelet, and advanced escapement technology.

Each Rolex is hand-assembled with strict quality control. The company even operates its own gold foundry, ensuring material excellence. This meticulous craftsmanship and long-lasting value make Rolex a global symbol of reliability, luxury, and status.
